About Us

OUR JURU INITIATIVE

Founded in 2021 by Ruhumuriza Justin, with aim of addressing different challenges that young people with disabilities face mainly in Rwanda.

We help young people with disabilities explore their potentials

JURU Initiative is an initiative created by youth and currently working in University of Rwanda Rwamagana campus. It aims at helping young people with disability through advocacy, reinforcement of capacity building, .

Promotion of sexual reproductive health, fighting gender-based violence against them and through promotion of inclusive education.

Ruhumuriza Justin

Our Mission

To take away hindrances that bother young people with disability in their route of building future.

Our Vision

JURU Initiative aims to build A world that believes that people with disabilities are capable and worthy.

Our Values

Integrity, Respect for human rights,Non profit empowerment,Embrace our community
